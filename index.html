About Us

Cat Got Your Croffle?

Bringing the Ultimate Pastry Mashup to H-Town

Welcome to Cat Got Your Croffle! We are a locally grown, Houston-based pop-up dedicated to two things: a beautiful, buttery obsession with croffles, and an unapologetic love for cats.

If you haven't experienced the magic yet, a croffle is what happens when the rich, flaky layers of a classic French croissant meet the golden, caramelized crunch of a waffle iron. It’s crispy on the outside, soft and airy on the inside, and completely irresistible.
